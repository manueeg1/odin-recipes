Tomato Soup

Tomato soup is a soup with tomatoes as the primary ingredient. It can be served hot or cold, and may be made in a variety of ways

Fun fact: Tomato soup is highly nutritious, rich in antioxidants, improves bone health, and may reduce heart disease

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Combine butter, onion, and tomatoes
  2. Add water or broth and simmer
  3. Blend
