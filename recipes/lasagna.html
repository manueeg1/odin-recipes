Lasagna

An Italian dish stacked with layers and layers of cheese

It is a delicous and popular dish among those that can tolerate lactose. Cheeses range from anything common like mozzarella and provolone and go as weird as ricotta.

Fun fact: there is a very similar dish hailing from Rome called lasana.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup mincen onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
